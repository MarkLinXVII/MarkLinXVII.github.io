Doing 1A Online at Waterloo

posted by Mark Lin on 12/29/2020

My experience at the University of Waterloo for my 1A classes was quite satisfactory. I was fortunate enough to be among the few selected to participate in the on-campus lectures of Math 135 and 137, and it made my learning more engaging and provided motivation for me to study, which many other students found difficulty acquiring.
