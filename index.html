Pixel Miner

During August 2019, I flew to San Jose for an internship at a startup blockchain company called DxChain Network/ My task was to create a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain that functioned as a game using ether as a currency in the three weeks I stayed. Due to the short time I had to write the contract and the limited knowledge I had to create it, I developed my time management skills and collaborative skills quite a bit. The game I made involved gambling ether in a pool using "pixel miners" which can be purchased by ether.
